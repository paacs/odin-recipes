Castagnaccio Pugliese (Italian Chestnut Flour Cake)

Description

This is my own take on a traditional italian recipe that I found shortly after I went gluten free. What started off as a compromise quickly proved to be popular with (non-GF) friends and family alike!

Ingredients

  • 1 1⁄2 cups chestnut flour
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1⁄2 cup olive or coconut oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • pinch of salt
  • 1⁄4 cup maple syrup or coconut sugar
  • a small handful of pine nuts/walnuts/pecans
  • dried ginger slices
  • (optional) acai powder)
  • (optional) cacao nibs

Preparation

  1. Preheat oven at 350°F/180°C.
  2. Grease a round 9 inch baking pan and cover the bottom with baking paper.
  3. Mix all of the ingredients in a bowl. Save some nuts to sprinkle over after baking. The mixture should be creamy but liquid.
  4. Pour the batter into the baking pan and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Dust with ground coconut or coconut sugar and serve warm.
