Sweet Potato Fries

Description

The perfect side of fries.

Ingredients

  • 1 large sweet potato per person
  • (optional) carrots to supplement fries
  • 2 tbsp avocado or coconut oil
  • turmeric
  • ground black pepper
  • harissa
  • rosemary
  • garlic powder
  • salt
  • almond flour

Preparation

  1. Peel sweet potato (and optional carrots) and slice into <1cm fries.
  2. Preheat air fryer
  3. Add fries to a large mixing bowl and drizzle with avocado or coconut oil, add spices, dust with almond flour, and toss until evenly coated.
  4. Air fry or bake at 400°F/200°C for 25 minutes, tossing every few minutes.
  5. Serve with wasabi mayonnaise
